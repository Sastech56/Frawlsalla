Viper Red

A woman mastered with extensive number of martial arts with her talented skills, Viper Red strongly seeked for a job connected to her martial arts skills and started career as guard.

Later on her skill was praised and assigned as body guard for high class personnels. After just a few years, Viper Red has joined PMC, the best of all connected industries. With her quick movement, she is the best in close combat as well as stealth operation.
