Game Background


Formation of Free Rebels

The growing number of immigrants who do not get jobs and are expelled from society, so as to survive the immigrants then commit a variety of crimes from robbery to drug trafficking. This crime developed into a organized movement until an organization called Free Rebels was formed. The goal is nothing but to control the entire drug and arms trade around the world and create fear for society.


Formation of CT-Force

As the conflict with immigrants intensified, the government decided to establish a special organization to deal with terrorists. Since the establishment of this organization, they began to seek information and whereabouts of a terrorist organization called Free Rebels. In line with the increasing terrorist threat, the government then sent the best troop assistance ever in the government that later came and joined and renamed CT-Force (Counter Terrorist Force).

Gameplay Description

In Frawlsalla, players join either the Free Rebels or CT-Force team. Each team attempts to complete their mission objective and/or eliminate the opposing team. Each round starts with the two teams spawning simultaneously, usually at opposite ends of the map from each other.

A player can choose to play as one of four different default character models in the game. Players are generally given a few seconds before the round begins, or before respawning, to change weapons and/or equipment.

Game points and experience are awarded for winning a round, losing a round, killing enemies, and completing mini missions.

Game Modes

  • Death Match

    • Kill players from the enemy until the score of one team reaches the specified score or becomes the team with the most score when the game time runs out.

  • Bomb Mission

    • The goal of the Free Rebels team is to detonate an area called a bombsite with C4, while the CT-Force team's goal is to prevent the Free Rebels team from detonating the bombsite.

  • Destroy Mission

    • Destroy the object that is targeted in this mode.

  • Madness

    • Finish off all the zombies and survive until time runs out.

  • Eliminate

    • Finish off all the enemies you have to win the round.

  • Shotgun Mode

    • Only shotgun-type weapons can be used in this mode.

  • Sniper Mode

    • Only sniper rifle type weapons can be used in this mode.

  • Defense Mission

    • Defend the object being targeted or destroy it.

  • Ally Mission

    • Survive the dinosaur attack andescapethrough the door.

  • Cross Counter

    • Fight dinosaurs until time runs out.

  • AI (Artificial Intelligence) Mode

    • Eliminate enemies controlled by AIl computer programs (not available in Indonesia).

  • Knuckle Mode

    • Finish off enemies using Bare Fist.

  • Head Hunter

    • Finish off all enemies with head shots only.

  • Chaos

    • Eliminate all enemies without the help of HUD and Crosshair, and all weapons have the same damage as in the real world.

  • Ace

    • Fight 1 vs 1 with other players up to several rounds with any weapon.

  • Step Up

    • Finish off enemies using weapons that change automatically.

  • Space

    • Finish off enemies and get Barrett M82A1 or RPG-7 in every box in the map. Use jump to jump high.

  • Convoy Mode

    • Free Rebels must seize the object and the CT-Force must defend the object from the Free Rebels.

  • Free for All

    • Finish off all the players and get the highest score. There are no teams/friends in this mode, all fighting each other.

  • Bomb Sniper

    • Play Bomb Mission mode only with Sniper Rifle class weapons.

  • Battle Cup (A Type of Battle Royale)

    • Survive by searching for weapons and items from boxes. The survivors will win.
