Acid Pol

After graduating from the Aegis Inc training school, Acid petitioned to join a special group of fighters trained in counter-guerilla warfare. While he is highly skilled in all aspects of combat and tactics, he is unfriendly and gruff, earning him his call sign "Acid."

Having come from a merchant background, Acid sides with the right of Aegis Inc and the other corporations who wish to take over the governance of their country.
